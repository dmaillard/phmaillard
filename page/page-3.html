    Among many foreigners watching the ethanol market, one of the most enthusiastic of all is the Indian Vinod Khosla, billionaire anchored in the United States and considered one of the most successful venture capitalists in the world. At 51 years of age, owner of a curriculum including the founding of Sun Microsystems and the financing of Google, as well as having diplomas in Biotechnology and a MBA from Stanford, Khosla is a super star of the business world, respected at Wall Street as well as in the Silicon Valley.
